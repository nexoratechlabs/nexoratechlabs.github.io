About

  • 2019

    The Spark of Tachyon

    Tachyon was founded in 2019 with a vision to explore the intersection of technology and innovation. What began as a small, passionate team of developers quickly grew into a startup driven by curiosity and a desire to solve real-world problems through software.

  • 2021

    A Shift Toward Hardware

    As the world of connected devices evolved, so did Tachyon’s mission. In 2021, the company pivoted its focus toward hardware solutions and embedded systems, diving deep into microcontrollers, IoT integrations, and edge computing technologies. This marked the beginning of a new chapter—bridging the physical and digital worlds.

  • April 2025

    The Birth of Nexora Tech Labs

    With years of experience and a bold new vision, Nexora Tech Labs was founded in 2025 as the evolution of Tachyon’s journey. Built on the foundation of creativity, engineering precision, and a passion for problem-solving, Nexora now stands as a modern software and hardware lab—committed to building smart, scalable, and meaningful tech for the future.

Smart Sensor Grid for Environmental Monitoring

A network of IoT sensors built with microcontrollers that collect temperature, humidity, and air quality data in real-time. Data is transmitted via LoRa/mesh network to a centralized dashboard for analysis and alerts. Optimized for low-power, remote environments.

Real-Time Object Detection on the Edge

An edge-optimized computer vision system using YOLO or MobileNet models, running directly on Raspberry Pi or NVIDIA Jetson. Capable of detecting objects in live video feed with low latency, ideal for automation, security, or robotics applications.

Secure OTA Update Framework for Embedded Devices

A secure over-the-air firmware update system with encrypted payloads, rollback protection, and version control. Built for microcontroller-based devices with limited memory, ensuring reliable updates even over unstable networks.
